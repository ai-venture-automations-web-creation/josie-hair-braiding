Our Story

Rooted in Culture,
Defined by Craft

At Josie Hair Braiding, we believe every braid is a bridge — connecting tradition, artistry, and identity. Located in the heart of Orange, NJ, we have built a reputation as the community's most trusted African hair braiding destination.

Our specialist brings years of hands-on mastery to every appointment. Whether you are protecting your natural hair, trying a new style, or keeping up a look you love, we give you the focused attention and skilled technique your hair deserves.

We take pride in low-tension methods that prioritize the health of your edges and scalp. Every client leaves looking and feeling their absolute best — and that commitment shows in our perfect 5-star rating.
